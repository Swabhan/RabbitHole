Rabbit Hole

Rabbit Hole offers a toolkit to utilize as you search through Google, providing features to organize searches, build rabbit holes, collaborate with others, manage tabs, encourage productivity, and share with the community!

+/Checkmark

Enters your current web page into our extension, allowing you to interact with the current page in the future

The Rabbit Icon

Allows you to choose your current rabbit hole while providing information on how you got to your current page.

Graph

See how your whole search experience is connected to one another, allowing you to organize and gain context on your current state.

Collaborate

Collaborate with others and share insights on your search journey.

Tabs

Manage your tabs effectively, allowing you to save your current tabs and open a previous set of tabs at any time.

Productivity

Leverage productivity tools to stay focused while navigating through Google search and using our extension.

Explore

Discover rabbit holes built by others while sharing your own to inspire and guide the community.
